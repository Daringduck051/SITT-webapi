Support Interaction Theme Tally

___

This website is designed to help a support agent count the number of interactions per theme happen during their shift.

In order to do so, the agent will click on the "+" button to add an interaction to the respective theme, and the "-" button to remove an interaction from the respective theme.

To add a custom theme, the agent can click on the "Add Custom Theme" button and fill out the necessary information.

To remove the custom theme, the agent can click on the three dots next to the custom theme and select "Remove Theme".

Agents can reset all counts to zero by clicking on the "Reset" or "New Shift" button.

Agents can email the tally data by going into "Summary Mode" and clicking on "Email".
